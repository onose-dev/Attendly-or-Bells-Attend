Grant essential permissions

Attendly needs two permissions to verify your attendance. Both are required for check-in to work.

Location Access
Confirms you are physically inside the lecture hall when checking in.
Not granted
Your GPS coordinates are compared to the registered coordinates of your lecture hall. The system only checks whether you are within 30–50 metres of the room — your location is never stored or shared beyond this check.
Notification Access
Alerts you the moment your lecturer opens an attendance session, even when the app is in the background.
Not granted
When your lecturer starts attendance for one of your courses, Attendly sends you a push notification so you can check in promptly — even if the app tab is closed. Without this, you may miss the check-in window.

Check-in will not work without location access. You can enable it later in your browser settings.
